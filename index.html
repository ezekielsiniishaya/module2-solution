My Life's Story

Love
I have been sad. I know the way of sorrow. My weaknesses made me welcome emptiness, for I felt I could not go beyound here. Not until I had a different feeling, one that is not about me. It changed me. It is changing me. Now I can go from here, I can love, for I am loved.
Learning
What a blessing it is to learn. I walked the crooked path that was laid for me. I had no choice, for I do not know any better, but nevertheless I am glad. Through the thick and thin, the hurt and pain, the shear force of life was all around me and I had no option but to learn.
Hope
Oh! That I may never loose hope. The dreams I have, the visions I see; how could I loose hope? These beautiful things, that eye have not seen, nor was it mentioned by any mouth, I long for the day that they would come. O, I believe.
